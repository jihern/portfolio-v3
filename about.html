About
Hi, my name is Ji-hern Baek, and I'm passionate about people. I want to learn everything I can about others - their idiosyncrasies, their stories. In doing so, I learn a lot about myself, too.

I am a Product Designer, finding joy in the intersection between people, design, and technology. Currently, I work as a Visual Design Intern for a marketing consulting firm and recently helped out with the two following projects: Ember and Leeo. Over the summer, I balanced my time as a UX Design Intern at a VR startup called FullDive and as a Styling & Marketing Intern at True&Co., a lingerie e-commerce startup.

I am also a fourth year at UC Berkeley, majoring in Cognitive Science (with a concentration in Society, Culture, and Cognition) and minoring in Computer Science. In addition to academics, I served as the president of the Korean American Student Association; was involved in an a cappella group; was a part of Innovative Design and Berkeley Innovation - two of Cal's design organizations; and currently work in our university's student government.
